## I am looking for MS programs. Do you have research positions available?
Please follow the official application process to CU Boulder. I do not have TA, RA, or any other paid positions available for MS students. 
If you are still interested, please take one of my courses, and then come talk to me about research opportunities.

## I am interested in doing a PhD in your lab. Are you accepting new students?
I am always looking for PhD students that would be a good fit for our group. Please follow the official application process to CU Boulder. 
You may also email me with details about your previous research and why you are interested in my group. If you are local, you are welcome to stop by during office hours 
to discuss further.
